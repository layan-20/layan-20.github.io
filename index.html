About me

Software Engineer

"I believe in a user-centered design approach — every project I touch is tailored to meet the specific needs of its users."

I'm Layan Jabr — a Software Engineer and Web Designer based in Charlotte, NC. My background spans both sides of the stack, which means I don't just make things look good. I understand why they work, and I fix them when they don't.

I approach every project through a lens of efficiency and precision. I don't chase trends — I ask why something works, then build it to last. Whether it's a WordPress rebuild for a local business, a performance audit, or a full-stack platform for a growing team, my process is always the same: understand the problem deeply, then execute cleanly.

I'm equally at home designing in Figma, building in Elementor, or debugging a Node.js API. I use AI tools like Claude, Cursor, and GitHub Copilot not to replace thinking, but to sharpen it. That combination makes me the person teams route their hardest problems to.
