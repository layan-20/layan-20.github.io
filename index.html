About me

"I don't just make things look good. I make them work."

I'm Layan Jabr — a Web Designer and Front-End Developer based in Charlotte, NC. My background is full-stack engineering, which means I'm as comfortable talking about database queries as I am about typography. That makes me different from most designers.

I build in WordPress and Elementor, obsess over Google Lighthouse scores, implement SEO from day one, and hand off sites that are production-ready — not just pretty mockups. I've collaborated with marketing teams, account managers, and QA engineers across three companies. I move fast without cutting corners.

I use AI tools including Claude and GitHub Copilot to work smarter — not to replace thinking, but to ship better work faster. I also design in Figma and Adobe Suite, create content in Canva, and speak both the language of developers and the language of clients.

I'm the person who stays curious about why something works, not just that it does.
